The palette

The six core colors above are the brand. Every asset draws from them. The four extended neutrals are tints and shades derived from the core hues — use them for depth, reversed text and large dark surfaces, never as a primary brand color.

Anatomy

Construction & clear space

  • LockupFlag-and-cup mark at left; fairway set over advisors.ai, right-aligned to a shared edge.
  • The accentThe ai in fairway and the .ai in advisors.ai are always terracotta. This is the one fixed rule of the wordmark.
  • AlignmentFlag top aligns to the cap of “f”. Cup bottom aligns to the “advisors.ai” baseline. Both text lines share the same right edge.
  • Clear spaceKeep clear space on all sides equal to the height of the lowercase “f”. Nothing intrudes.

05
Assets

Signature graphics

Beyond the mark, the brand carries a small family of built graphics that extend the golf metaphor into product and marketing. Reuse them as-is, or hand the source to your AI tools to generate variants — always in the locked palette.

The signature asset

The RangeFinder

A zoomed-green view through a rangefinder reticle: the logo’s flag and pin sit on the green’s slope contours, framed by a terracotta target bracket, with yardage-to-pin and slope-to-green readouts in the mono face. Anchors the RangeFinder offering and any “measure before you swing” moment — diagnostics, assessments, discovery. Keep the lightweight feel; never add colours beyond the palette.

Simplified reticle mark
A compact reticle-and-pin lockup for small slots — capability cards, list markers, favicons and app icons. Forest on light, cream on dark.
Crawl · Walk · Run
The AI-maturity journey mapped to the course — Tee Box (crawl) → Fairway (walk) → Green (run), with the pin waiting on the green. Use for roadmaps, maturity framing and phase storytelling.
06
In the wild

Letterhead vs. golf shirt

Letterhead Full color

Use the Primary lockup (forest · terracotta · taupe) on white or cream stock, top-left, ~38 mm wide, over a short terracotta rule. Paper reproduces all four ink colors cleanly, so the full-color mark is the richest, most “advisory” choice here.

Keep the rest of the page quiet: forest body text, taupe contact line, generous margins.

Golf shirt 1–2 color

Embroidery favors few thread colors at small left-chest size. Forest polo → monochrome cream mark is the cleanest, most premium option. On stone/sand fabric, use the monochrome forest mark.

If you want the accent to read, a two-color forest + terracotta stitch works — but avoid full four-color embroidery at chest scale.

07
Guardrails

Please don’t

×

Recolor the accentThe “ai / .ai” stays terracotta. Never swap it for another hue.

×

Change the typeDon’t substitute the typeface, weights, or re-space the letters.

×

Distort or rotateScale proportionally only. No stretching, skewing or tilting.

×

Low contrastDon’t place forest text on forest, or sand on sand. Use a treatment built for the surface.

×

Outside colorsNever introduce hues beyond this palette. Stay in sand, terracotta, forest, taupe.

×

Crowd the markRespect the clear space. Keep type, edges and imagery out of it.

×

Logo integrityNever show the core brand logo in any stretched or altered layout. The left axis of “fairway” and the left axis of “advisors.ai” must always align — and so must their right edges; the two lines stay locked on both axes. The golf flag, hole and ball always sit just to the left of the wordmark. The two layouts below are wrong.
